Hi, I'm Nadeen

Applied Mathematician & Systems Designer

I am driven by the transition point where abstract concepts take shape in the real world. To me, this is a mathematical challenge. With a background in Applied Mathematics, I approach the world through mechanistic modeling and simulation to understand the “why” behind complex system behavior.

I find my best work at the intersection of high-stakes technical constraints and deep ambiguity. I am drawn to the friction that occurs when diverse disciplines such as biology, computation, and policy integrate. My interest lies in bridging these gaps to build something greater than the sum of its parts.

Whether utilizing digital twins to optimize physical structures or designing the infrastructure for translational science, my goal is to build the frameworks that turn breakthrough ideas into durable realities. I do not just want to solve the problem in front of me; I want to refine the engine that drives it.
